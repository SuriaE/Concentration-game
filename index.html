HOME

Welcome to this page. Here you can find the memory game popularly known as Concentration game. This is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards. Concentration can be played with any number of players or as solitaire. It is a particularly good game for young children, though adults may find it challenging and stimulating as well.

INSTRUCTIONS

1.Mix up the cards.

2.Lay them in rows, face down.

3.Turn over any two cards.

4.If the two cards match, keep them.

5.If they don't match, turn them back over.

6.Remember what was on each card and where it was.

7.The game is over when all the cards have been matched.

8.Play again by clicking on the START button
